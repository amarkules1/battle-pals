# Battle Pals Plot

## Main Character

User will pick a name and gender for the player character. They'll be a university student and biology researcher who has just moved to Paltopia from Liberty City. Their initial goal is to explore the country of Paltopia and document the various species of Pals that live there. 

## Paltopia

Paltopia is a country divided into 9 precincts, with each precinct having a unique environment and set of Pals. The player will start in the starting precinct and will need to travel to each precinct to document the various species of Pals that live there. Paltopia is much different from liberty city in 2 ways. For one, there are no Battle Pals in Liberty City, whereas they're abundant in Paltopia. Secondly, Paltopia has an authoritarian government, who's stated goal is to protect its citizens from the more dangerous Pals in the country. In reality, the government and its leader are corrupt and are using the Pals as a way to control the population. Weakly tie the 2 to Hong Kong (Liberty City), and Mainland China (Paltopia).

## Progression

The player will progress through the different precincts, learning about and capturing the Pals that live there. Travel between precincts is largely restricted though, and in order to gain access to new ones they need to defeat the top beaurocrat in each, culminating in a fight against the ruler of the country to earn the people the right to interact with battle pals how they wish.
