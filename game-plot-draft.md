# Battle Pals Plot

## Main Character

User will pick a name and gender for the player character. They'll be a university student and biology researcher who has just moved to Paltopia from Liberty City. Their initial goal is to explore the country of Paltopia and document the various species of Pals that live there. 

## Paltopia

Paltopia is a country divided into 9 precincts, with each precinct having a unique environment and set of Pals. The player will start in the starting precinct and will need to travel to each precinct to document the various species of Pals that live there. Paltopia is much different from liberty city in 2 ways. For one, there are no Battle Pals in Liberty City, whereas they're abundant in Paltopia. Secondly, Paltopia has an authoritarian government, who's stated goal is to protect its citizens from the more dangerous Pals in the country. In reality, the government and its leader are corrupt and are using the Pals as a way to control the population. Weakly tie the 2 to Hong Kong (Liberty City), and Mainland China (Paltopia).

## Progression

The player will progress through the different precincts, learning about and capturing the Pals that live there. Travel between precincts is largely restricted though, and in order to gain access to new ones they need to defeat the top bureaucrat in each, culminating in a fight against the ruler of the country to earn the people the right to interact with battle pals how they wish.

## Mechanics

- User gets to choose one of three starter pals at the beginning of the game, gifted to him by a professor who's sending him off to Paltopia to study the local wildlife. 
- Professor will also give the usercapture cubes that they can use to capture wild pals. Capture cubes can only be used at the end of a battle when the user's pal has won the battle. 
- Pals gain experience from battle in order to level up, gain new moves, and evolve.
- Wild pals can be found throughout the world. Advancing in the game requires the player to go through areas where wild pals exist. Each precint has a specialty type, but pals of other types can sometimes be found there too.
- Labs can be found in each precinct. In a lab, the user can heal their pals, get additional capture cubes and other supplies, and access the pal storage system to exchange pals into and out of their party. They can have 10 pals in their party at a time.
- Some people throughout the world will battle the user on site, with bureaucrats and their minions being some of the toughest battles in the game. 
- User can travel between precincts they have alredy unlocked, but travel to new ones is restricted until they defeat the bureaucrat in the current one. Fast travel between precincts can happen at the north, south, east, and west gates of each precinct.
